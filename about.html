I started riding dressage when I was 12 years old, and fell in love with it. Since then, I have tried my hand at fox hunting and eventing, but it doesn't compare to the challenge and satisfaction I get from dressage. I have shown through 4th level on self trained horses, earning my USDF Bronze Medal and half of my Silver. I since have gained experience schooling through Grand Prix movements and am actively working to earn both my Silver and Gold medals.

I have recently acquired two fabulous KWPN foals from Scot and Carol Tolman of Shooting Star Farm. Neo SSF (Don Olymbrio x UB-40 x Pass the Glass XX) pictured right and Olympites SSF, aka Olie, (Secret x Donatelli x Jazz) pictured left. They are both fabulous sport and breeding prospects, and I look forward to sharing my journey with them on this site!
